# Editorial Principles

This repository should remain:

- structured enough for machine consumption
- clear enough for legal, compliance, and product teams
- practical enough for operational implementation
- careful not to overstate legal certainty

## Writing Rules

- Prefer short sections and stable headings.
- Separate legal references from operational guidance.
- Mark assumptions and interpretation layers explicitly.
- Avoid unsupported legal claims.
- Keep templates implementation-oriented.

## Machine-Readability Rules

- Preserve consistent front matter where used.
- Keep filenames descriptive and stable.
- Update [`framework/simpleact-framework.json`](../framework/simpleact-framework.json) when major sections change.
- Keep [`llms.txt`](../llms.txt) aligned with the main entry points.
