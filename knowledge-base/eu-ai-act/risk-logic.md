# Risk Logic

Within the SimpleAct framework, risk logic must be explicit and repeatable. Companies should not guess their way through AI classification.

The repository distinguishes at least four decision layers:

- prohibited practices screening
- high-risk relevance screening
- transparency and user information duties
- residual governance risk and internal control needs

This keeps regulatory categorization separate from broader governance judgment.

That distinction matters. A system may fall outside formal high-risk categories and still require strong internal governance because of decision impact, sensitive context, or operational dependency.
