# Framework Overview

The SimpleAct AI Governance Framework is a practical operating model for AI compliance and AI governance.

It is designed to help organizations move from scattered policy discussions to a documented and repeatable system of:

- AI system identification
- role and ownership definition
- risk classification
- control implementation
- documentation management
- monitoring and review

## Framework Objectives

- establish a reliable inventory of AI systems and use cases
- align governance responsibilities across legal, compliance, product, and technical teams
- map operational controls to regulatory expectations
- create evidence that can support audits, reviews, or internal approvals
- support continuous oversight as systems evolve

## Intended Users

- compliance teams
- founders and operators
- product leaders
- engineering and ML teams
- procurement and vendor management functions

## Core Components

- governance model
- lifecycle model
- control domains
- templates and records
- review and monitoring process
